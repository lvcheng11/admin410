Financing

DHP accepts any kind of credit cards and debit card. And we also accept paypal through online payment.

Delivery

DHP uses a Third Party Logisitics company called UPS for its shipments and offers a flat rate ground shipping on all orders shipped to a valid US address (excluding Hawaii and Alaska). When placing your order, select your preferred delivery option before proceeding to purchase.

Return Policies

Once we receive your package, we will process your return within 3 business days. Print the provided return label. Affix the label to the outside of the shipping box. Drop off the package at the any Carrier Store, Drop Box, Customer Center, or with any Carrier driver.
